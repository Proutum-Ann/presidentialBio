After his final term as president, Clinton still kept in touch with the political world, whether it be through support or affiliation. He currently still works as a lawyer, politician, and author!

Hillary Clinton's 2008 Campaign

Not many years later, Bill Clinton's wife, Hillary, ran for Democrat nominee for the presidential election, but would be outshined by future president Barack Obama.

The Hurricane/Tsunami Funds (feat. Bush)

During both hurricane Katrina and as well as a tsunami, former presidents Clinton and Bush banded together to host the relief funds, Bush-Clinton Katrina Fund and Bush-Clinton Tsunami Fund.

William J. Clinton Foundation

A nonprofit organization that aims to "strengthen the capacity of people in the United States and throughout the world to meet the challenges of global interdependence."

The foundation was founded in 2001, the year after Clinton's final term as president, and it still holds up to this day, helping others who may be less fortunate than the elite in society.
